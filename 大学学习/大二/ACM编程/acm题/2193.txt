Kandy and Monica are good friends, and they both like playing one old stone game. In the game, there are N stones on the table at first. And the two players take turns to take stones from the table, and each player can take at least 1 stone and at most M stones each time. The winner is the one who takes the last stone. Kandy and Monica have played a lot of times, and they are both good players. When playing game, they both use perfect strategies. 
For any given value of N, Kandy wonder whether he can win the game or not, if Kandy goes first every time. Can you give him the answers? 
Input
The input will consist of several lines; each line consists of two integers N (0 < N < 231), the number of stones on the table initially, and M (1 ¡Ü M ¡Ü N), the number of stones that can be taken at most each time. The end of input is indicated by a line consists of a single number 0.
Output
For each game, if Kandy can win, prints "Just Do It.", otherwise prints "Think About It." in a single line. 
Sample Input
110 10
51 3
1985 11
0
Sample Output
Think About It.
Just Do It.
Just Do It.
